Who are we?

We are Southern Californians who share a passion for railroading, especially G-gauge Garden Railways.

Some of our members have more than 25 years experience in building garden railways for both public displays and private residences.

We also have relative new-comers to the hobby who are just planning their own garden railways.

SoCalGRS holds many open houses each year to showcase member layouts and to encourage others to get involved.
