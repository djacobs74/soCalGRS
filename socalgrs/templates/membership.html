Membership Information

Benefits of becoming a member:
  • Sharing Expertise
  • Family membership
  • SoCalGRS badges
  • Newsletters & archive
  • Event Participation
  • Frequent Member Garden Railroad Open Houses
  • Periodic Technical Clinics

To become a member of SoCalGRS, follow the three simple steps outlined below:

  1. Print out an application form
  2. Fill in the requested information on the form
  3. Mail completed application form and check to Ron Deisler, Treasurer of SoCalGRS (address is shown on application form)

Membership Renewal...it's never too late.

  • Print out a Renewal Form and follow #s 2 & 3 above.
